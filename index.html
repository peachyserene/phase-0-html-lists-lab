Griling a Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Instructions

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. BAdd cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
